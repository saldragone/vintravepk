Vinnie Trav

South Jersey • Country • Rock • Americana

Vinnie Trav is a singer-songwriter based in South New Jersey, bringing high-energy original music and crowd-favorite covers to stages across the Philadelphia and South Jersey region. Performing as a trio and full band, he delivers an authentic, feel-good experience rooted in country, rock, and Americana.

  • 40+ shows booked for 2026 across South NJ & the Philly region
  • Venues: The Mill Tavern, Bourbon & Brews, MudHen Brewing, Autumn Lake Winery
  • Festivals: Gloucester County 4-H Fair, Salem County Fair, Melodies on Main St
  • Latest release: “The Longer it Goes”
